About Stray Sense

Learn about our mission, vision, and the team behind our innovative approach to rabies prevention.

Our Story

From Idea to Impact

Founded in 2020, Stray Sense began as a university project aimed at solving the growing problem of rabies in urban areas. Our founders, a group of veterinary students and computer science engineers, recognized the potential of combining their expertise to create a technological solution.

What started as a simple drone surveillance system has evolved into a comprehensive platform integrating machine learning, community engagement, and veterinary science to create a sustainable solution for rabies prevention.

Today, we operate in 12 cities across 3 countries, with plans to expand our reach to more communities in need.

Our Mission

Technology for Good

Our mission is to eliminate rabies through early detection, prevention, and community education. We believe technology should serve humanity and the environment, which is why we've developed our innovative drone surveillance system.

By combining AI-powered analysis with veterinary expertise, we can identify potential rabies cases before they become outbreaks, saving both human and animal lives.

We're committed to creating solutions that are scalable, sustainable, and accessible to communities that need them most.
