Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

How do I report a stray dog in my area?

You can report stray dogs through our mobile app, website contact form, or by calling your local Stray Sense office. Please provide as much detail as possible about the location and behavior of the dog.

What should I do if I find an injured stray dog?

Do not approach the dog if it appears aggressive. Call our emergency line or local animal control. If the dog is approachable and you can safely transport it, take it to the nearest partner veterinary clinic.

How can I volunteer with Stray Sense?

We welcome volunteers in various capacities including field operations, community education, and administrative support. Visit our Volunteer page or contact your local office for opportunities in your area.

What's the adoption process like?

Our adoption process involves an application, home check, and meet-and-greet with the dog. All our dogs are vaccinated, microchipped, and spayed/neutered before adoption.

How accurate is your rabies detection technology?

Our AI-powered detection system has a 92% accuracy rate in identifying potential rabies symptoms. All detections are verified by our veterinary team before any action is taken.
