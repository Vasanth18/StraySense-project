Veterinarian Involvement

At Stray Sense, we believe that the expertise of veterinarians is crucial to the success of our mission. Our collaboration with veterinary professionals ensures the highest standard of care and health monitoring for stray dogs.

Veterinarians play a vital role in analyzing the health and behavior reports generated from user uploaded videos. They provide expert insights and recommendations based on these assessments, ensuring accurate and reliable information for pet owners and local authorities.

In cases where our drone technology detects potentially rabid dogs, veterinarians are immediately alerted to intervene. Their swift response is essential in managing and controlling rabies outbreaks, safeguarding both the community and stray dog populations.

Adoption Process

At Stray Sense, we ensure the well-being of adopted dogs by closely monitoring adoption facilities to maintain the highest standards of care. Our veterinarians conduct regular health checks and inspections to ensure cleanliness, safety, and comfort for all dogs.

We provide ongoing training and support to facility staff on various aspects of dog care, including nutrition, behavior management, and medical attention. Additionally, we encourage feedback from adopters to continuously improve the adoption experience.

This comprehensive approach ensures that every adopted dog receives the love, attention, and care they deserve, fostering a positive and supportive environment for their new beginnings.
